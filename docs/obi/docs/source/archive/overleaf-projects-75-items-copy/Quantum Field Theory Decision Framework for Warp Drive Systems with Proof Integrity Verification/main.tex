\begin{abstract}
This paper presents a rigorous mathematical framework for quantum coherence-based warp drive propulsion integrated with a proof integrity verification system. We formalize the coherence operator mechanics in Hilbert space, establish negative energy containment protocols through vacuum separation, and introduce a novel proof integrity matrix for safety-critical decision validation. The framework achieves Truth Weight (TW) scores $\geq 3.0$ through coherence-driven decision (CDD) mechanisms, ensuring no catastrophic failures in dimensional spacetime manipulation.
\end{abstract}

\section{Introduction}

Traditional warp drive proposals rely on exotic negative energy densities that violate known physical constraints.
